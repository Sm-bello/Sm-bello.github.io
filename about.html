Operational Imperatives

Establishing sovereign aerospace AI systems inside African airspace lines.

My technical operations experience across dual field layers—including data audit tracks at the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority (NCAA) Department of Airworthiness Standards and hands-on maintenance sprints at the Executive Airlift Group (EAG 307) hangar performing King Air 350 system inspections—exposed systemic vulnerabilities within regional operations.
