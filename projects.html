Physics‑Informed Avionics Security

  • → Physics‑Informed DT – four detection layers: static range, cross‑parameter, temporal delta, ADS‑B spoofing
  • → 100% attack detection – catches spoofed positions, impossible engine states, fuel fraud, ADS‑B injection
