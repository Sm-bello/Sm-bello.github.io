LIVE PROOF OF CONCEPT

AI for Fluids (Neural CFD)

Revolutionizing computational fluid dynamics by replacing traditional Navier-Stokes solvers with Physics-Informed Neural Operators (PINNs). The goal is to achieve real-time aerodynamic predictions on arbitrary geometries.

Proof of Concept Status: This interactive engine currently utilizes data from Aerosandbox/XFoil for real-time demonstration. The final deployment will transition to a high-fidelity ANSYS Fluent backend for industrial-grade predictions.

  • Deep Learning for instantaneous lift/drag coefficient prediction
  • Mesh-free flow field reconstruction using neural networks
  • NPU-certified low-speed aerodynamics foundation
  • GPU-accelerated inference for parametric design optimization
