High-Fidelity Landing Gear Digital Twin

A distributed Cyber-Physical System (CPS) architecture. Features a Python-based "Robot Pilot" (FMS) that autonomously injects faults into a high-fidelity Simscape Multibody physics plant. Real-time performance metrics are streamed via UDP to an InfluxDB 3 data warehouse for predictive health monitoring.
